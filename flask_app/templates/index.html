How to use Moby Data

Moby Data is an exploratory data analysis tool that scrapes data from OOI hydrophone recordings. With Moby Data, you can simply select a range of days and retrieve the desired audio recordings along with auto-generated figures to visualize the recording data and related statistics.


As part of this process, we extract the underlying latent space of the recording data based on the larger OOI database. This allows to infer what recordings are most similar to your recording, detect anomalies, or predict whether a whale call likely exists within a recording.


About the Project

This project was authored by Catherine Rasgaitis as part of the OCEAN 411 (Seagoing Research & Discovery) course. Catherine was also a part of the VISIONS 2024 field expedition where she lived and worked aboard the R/V Atlantis to assist in data collection and other wet lab activities, such as constructing water flow instruments, taking push core samples, and logging sea life.
